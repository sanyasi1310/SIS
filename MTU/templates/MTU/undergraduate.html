Undergraduate Admissions


MTU is committed to creating better opportunities for Indigenous students, building and improving respectful partnerships with Indigenous communities and encouraging everyone to be part of a shared vision for the future as part of our response to Canada's Truth and Reconciliation Commission. Learn in an amazing place with world-class facilities and instructors, unique programs and diverse perspectives create our extraordinary academic environment. Here, you'll learn by doing. Peer through microscopes, gaze at the stars, dig into the past, and contemplate the future with hands-on experience.


You could meet your mentor and learn from award-winning professors while getting involved in research as early as your first year of study. With our advanced facilities, leading experts and mentors, and funding resources-it's never been a better time to get involved in groundbreaking research. While our research spans multiple fields, we have key strengths in various areas that you should explore.

By building work experience into our degrees, our graduates are some of the most employable in the world. All of our direct-entry have some form of co-op, practicum or internship. Get a leg up in the job market and test drive your potential career. We have co-ops, practicums, and work experience programs in every faculty that can give you relevant work experience and connect you with employers in your field.

Over $32 million in scholarships, awards, and financial support is available to undergraduates each year. You could be starting with scholarships you don't even have to apply for! You will be evaluated for these scholarships when you apply for admission. Your eligibility will be based on your high school admission average at the time of your admission offer.
