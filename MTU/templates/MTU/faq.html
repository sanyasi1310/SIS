Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ - Admissions

Once you have submitted your application to MTU, you are no longer able to make changes online. If you made a mistake (i.e. name is misspelled, self-reporting grades mistakes, adding or removing extenuating circumstance, etc), you must submit a webform request.

Once you have submitted your application, it's too late to change your program choice. If you would like to make a program change or add a second choice, you will have to submit a new application before the application deadline.

If you didn't enter your OUAC number, you should submit it through online portal (Applicant Menu->Submit OUAC Reference Number). If the number you entered is incorrect, you can send the correct number through our webform.

FAQ - Winter 2021 and Fall 2021 entry

In response to COVID-19, both the IELTS and TOEFL tests have been made available online for students who are unable to take the tests at a test center. Please see the information on their respective websites

Electronic copies of official transcripts and proofs of degree can be sent to officialschooldocs@mtu.ca.

Acceptance deadlines vary by department for graduate studies. We recommend that you contact the graduate program director in your department. If the deadline has passed, you can request an extension. We are asking departments to be flexible given the circumstances.

Winter 2021 courses will be delivered primarily through remote platforms, with enhanced in-person teaching activities in accordance with public health protocols.
